The Yorkie2Life Ethos

We bring joy through sharing the love of our past and future generations of Yorkshire Terriers .

Therapeutic and Community Services

From mental and physical disabilities to exposure therapy for those in need of love from man's best friend, Yorkie2Life is here to provide support services to all in our community. We offer a full range of services catered to the needs of our clients, including time with our furry friends!

Rescue and Rehabilitation

Yorkie2Life is proud to be a rescue and rehabilitation partner with local and state animal shelters, including assistance with dogs requiring a high standard of love and care. Feel free to read more about our efforts, get involved, or see our rescued forever friends!

Puppies

We are often asked about puppy availability. Yorkie2Life IS NOT A DOG BREEDING SERVICE. Occasionally one of our friends may have a litter with 1-2 puppies available. Litter availability will be posted on the site, along with all the relevant pictures. Please send contact and household information, a description of the current living situation the new dog would be entering, and any other relevant information through our site.

Yorkie MidWife

If you or someone you know is in need of assistance delivering puppies, please contact us. We have over 20 years of experience with varied breeds, situations, and sizes. Let us help keep your forever friend's special moment safe and the memory you and your family deserve.
